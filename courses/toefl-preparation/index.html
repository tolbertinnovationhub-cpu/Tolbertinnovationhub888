Course Structure

TOEFL Preparation Course

Prepare effectively for TOEFL with focused skill-building and proven exam strategies.

This TOEFL Preparation Course is designed for learners who want to improve their performance in all sections of the TOEFL exam. The course combines language development with test-focused methods so learners can manage time, understand instructions, and respond with confidence.

Students will strengthen academic listening and reading comprehension, produce organized writing responses, and improve spoken answers for independent and integrated tasks. Practice activities are aligned with common TOEFL question formats.

By the end of the course, learners will be equipped to approach the TOEFL exam with better accuracy, stronger communication skills, and improved test readiness.

Course Modules

  • Module 1 – Listening Comprehension

Practice understanding academic lectures and conversations while identifying main ideas, supporting details, and speaker intent.

  • Module 2 – Reading Analysis

Build the skills to analyze passages, interpret vocabulary in context, and answer evidence-based reading questions effectively.

  • Module 3 – Integrated Writing

Learn how to organize ideas from reading and listening sources into clear, coherent written responses with proper structure.

  • Module 4 – Speaking Responses

Improve fluency, pronunciation, and response organization for both independent and integrated TOEFL speaking tasks.
