Course Structure

Business Startup Training

Learn how to turn business ideas into sustainable and profitable ventures.

This Business Startup Training course is designed for aspiring entrepreneurs and small business owners who want practical guidance to launch and grow a business. The course focuses on real-world startup decisions and simple systems that improve business performance.

Students will learn how to validate ideas, organize operations, attract customers, and manage finances with clarity. The training also introduces planning tools that help businesses scale responsibly and adapt to market changes.

By the end of the course, learners will have a clear startup roadmap and the confidence to make strategic business decisions.

Course Modules

  • Module 1 – Business Idea Validation

Assess business ideas based on customer needs, market demand, and practical feasibility before launch.

  • Module 2 – Operations and Customer Service

Learn how to organize daily operations, improve service quality, and build strong customer relationships.

  • Module 3 – Marketing and Sales Planning

Create marketing and sales strategies that attract customers, increase visibility, and drive revenue growth.

  • Module 4 – Budgeting and Growth Strategy

Build simple budgets, track performance, and design growth plans for long-term business sustainability.
