IELTS Mock Test — Answers & Marking Guide (sample)

Listening: 40 questions — sample guidance
- Check answers against listening transcript and mark correct = 1 point each.

Reading: 40 questions — sample guidance
- Allocate 1 point per correct answer; use answer key for passages.

Writing: Marking guidance (band descriptors summary)
- Task Achievement / Response: Address all parts and present clear overview (Task 1) or position (Task 2).
- Coherence and Cohesion: Use paragraphs and logical connectors.
- Lexical Resource: Use varied vocabulary; collocation accuracy.
- Grammatical Range and Accuracy: Use a mix of sentence structures with accurate grammar.

Speaking: Marking guidance (band descriptors summary)
- Fluency and Coherence: Speak at length without undue hesitation.
- Lexical Resource: Use appropriate vocabulary.
- Grammatical Range and Accuracy: Use varied grammar accurately.
- Pronunciation: Be intelligible and use some features of connected speech.

Notes
- This file provides a basic guidance template. For official scoring, refer to IELTS public band descriptors and trained examiners.
