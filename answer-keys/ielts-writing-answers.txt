IELTS Writing — Example Responses & Tips

Task 1 (Sample report)
Question: The chart shows the number of students studying abroad from 2010 to 2020. Describe the main trends.

Sample Response:
The chart illustrates the number of students studying abroad between 2010 and 2020. Overall, the number of international students increased steadily during this period. In 2010, approximately 20,000 students studied abroad. This number rose significantly to nearly 50,000 by 2020.

Task 2 (Model essay plan)
Question: Some people believe that technology improves education, while others think it has negative effects. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

Model Outline:
- Introduction: Paraphrase topic and state balanced view.
- Body 1: Benefits of technology (access, engagement, resources) with examples.
- Body 2: Drawbacks (distraction, inequality, reduced face-to-face interaction).
- Conclusion: State opinion and briefly summarize points.

Writing Tips
- Use clear paragraphing and linking phrases.
- Support ideas with examples.
- Proofread for grammar and vocabulary variety.
