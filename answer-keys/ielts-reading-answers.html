IELTS Reading Practice — Answer Key

Practice passage: The Importance of Technology in Education

1) What has transformed education?
   - Sample answer: Technology has transformed education.

2) What tools are used to improve teaching methods?
   - Sample answer: Computers and internet tools are used to improve teaching methods.

3) How can teachers communicate with students?
   - Sample answer: Teachers can communicate with students through digital platforms.
