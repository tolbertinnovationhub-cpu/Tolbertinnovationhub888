IELTS Speaking — Sample Answers & Prompts

Part 1 — Introduction and Interview (examples)
Q: What is your hometown like?
A: My hometown is a small city with friendly people and a growing market. It has a few parks and good local schools.

Part 2 — Long Turn (cue card example)
Cue Card: Describe a book you recently read.
Sample Points: The book was about leadership; I read it to improve my management skills; I learned practical communication techniques.

Part 3 — Discussion (sample answers)
Q: Why is reading important for students?
A: Reading improves vocabulary, critical thinking, and knowledge retention, which support academic success.

Tips
- Speak clearly and organize your ideas.
- Use a range of vocabulary and grammar.
- Expand answers in Part 3 with reasons and examples.
