TOEFL Speaking Practice Questions

Independent Speaking Examples:
1) Do you prefer studying alone or in groups? Explain why.
2) Describe a place you would like to visit and why.

Integrated Speaking Examples:
1) Read a short passage about a campus policy, listen to a speaker discussing it, then summarize how the speaker responds to the reading.
2) Read about a campus program, listen to a student describing their experience, then explain the relationship.

Timing notes:
- Independent tasks: ~15 seconds preparation, 45 seconds response.
- Integrated tasks: short prep (30–45 seconds) and ~60 seconds response depending on task.
