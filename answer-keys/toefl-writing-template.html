TOEFL Writing Template

Integrated Writing (structure):
- Introduction: briefly state the relationship between reading and lecture
- Summary of the reading: key points
- Explanation of the lecture: main points that relate or contradict the reading
- Comparison/Conclusion: explain how the lecture supports or challenges the reading

Independent Essay (structure):
- Introduction: present position and outline main reasons
- Body Paragraph 1: reason + example
- Body Paragraph 2: reason + example
- Conclusion: restate position and summarize reasons

Tips:
- Use clear paragraphing and transition words
- Support points with examples
- Stick to the word limit and proofread
