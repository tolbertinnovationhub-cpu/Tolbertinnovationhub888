IELTS SPEAKING PRACTICE QUESTIONS

Part 1 (Personal)
1. Tell me about your hometown.
2. What do you enjoy doing in your free time?
3. How do you usually spend weekends?

Part 2 (Cue Card)
Describe a skill you recently learned.
- What it is
- How you learned it
- Why it is important

Part 3 (Discussion)
1. Why are communication skills important today?
2. How can students improve speaking confidence?
3. What is the role of technology in language learning?
