Study Abroad Application Timeline Planner

6-9 months before deadline:
- Define country and program goals
- Shortlist universities/scholarships

4-6 months before deadline:
- Prepare transcripts and credentials
- Draft personal statement
- Contact referees for recommendations

2-4 months before deadline:
- Finalize documents
- Submit applications
- Track confirmation emails

1-2 months before deadline:
- Prepare for interviews
- Prepare visa and financial documents
